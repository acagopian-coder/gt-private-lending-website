About GT

Built by enthusiasts.
Engineered for performance.

GT was born from a lifelong obsession. Our founders have been amateur racing drivers since childhood — the kind of people who grew up studying lap times, dissecting suspension geometry, and chasing the perfect corner exit. That love for the flawless engineering of high-performance machines became the lens through which we built everything.

We brought that same relentless pursuit of precision to private lending. GT is a Florida-based, nationwide direct lender — operating in partnership with the highest and most flexible institutional capital available. We don't just place loans. We engineer them.

With over 10 years in the industry and more than $2 billion funded across our previous ventures, we are veterans who know this market from every angle — and we built GT to be the lender we always wished existed.
